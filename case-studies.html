Forecasting & Demand Planning

Time Series Decomposition & Promotional Impact

IIF Certified | Lancaster University CMAF — Task 1

Decomposed daily sales data to identify trend, seasonality, and promotional effects. Used the ADAM framework with external regressors to quantify promotion impact and detect cannibalization.

Key Results

  • Promo1: +36% sales uplift
  • Promo2: +16% sales uplift
  • Combined: -76% (cannibalization detected)
