Forecasting & Demand Planning

Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimization

IIF Certified Project | Lancaster University CMAF

Comprehensive forecasting analysis of 20 consumer goods products across four demand types (smooth, variable, erratic, intermittent). Applied ETS, Croston, SBA, and TSB methods with rolling origin evaluation. Developed a hierarchical method selection framework (Bias → RMSE → AICc) and calculated safety stock for 97% service level.

Key Skills

  • Time Series Decomposition
  • Rolling Origin Evaluation (15 origins, 7-day horizon)
  • Intermittent Demand Methods
  • Safety Stock Calculation
  • R Programming

Promotional Impact Analysis

IIF Certified Project | Lancaster University CMAF

Analyzed the impact of promotional activities on daily sales using time series decomposition and the ADAM framework with external regressors. Quantified individual promotion effects and identified cannibalization when promotions overlap.

Key Findings

  • Promo 1: +36% sales uplift
  • Promo 2: +16% sales uplift
  • Combined: -24% (cannibalization effect)
