Certifications

Certified Professional Forecaster (CPF)

Institute of Business Forecasting & Planning (IBF)

The CPF certification validates advanced expertise in demand forecasting and planning, covering statistical methods, forecast process design, performance measurement, and organizational integration of forecasting functions.

CPIM - Production & Inventory Management

ASCM (Association for Supply Chain Management)

CPIM certification demonstrates comprehensive knowledge of production planning, master scheduling, MRP/MPS, capacity planning, and inventory management principles aligned with industry best practices.

My Approach

I believe effective analytics requires more than just technical skills. My approach combines:

Rigor

Sound methodology, proper validation, and honest assessment of uncertainty. I use rolling origin evaluation, bias testing, and multiple accuracy metrics to ensure forecasts are reliable.

Clarity

Complex analysis means nothing if stakeholders can't understand it. I prioritize clear communication, actionable recommendations, and business-focused insights over technical jargon.

Impact

Analysis should drive decisions. I focus on business outcomes — reduced inventory costs, improved service levels, better planning accuracy — not just technical metrics.
